# Laptop Variant

## Target profile

The laptop variant is intended for:

- mobile research workflows
- field-edge analysis
- portable engineering environments
- low-power cognitive computing

## Hardware constraints

The laptop form factor requires:

- compact cooling systems
- efficient power management
- reduced memory and bus complexity
- strong integration between CPU, GPU, and NPU components

## Architecture

The laptop design applies the same quasicrystalline idea at a smaller scale:

1. A compact core fabric with a small number of strongly connected local clusters
2. A sparse but adaptive interconnect that favors low-energy routing
3. Dynamic offloading of heavy tasks to a local accelerator or remote edge node

## Expected benefits

- lower thermal stress
- improved energy efficiency under burst workloads
- graceful degradation when one module becomes unavailable
- better portability without losing structural resilience
